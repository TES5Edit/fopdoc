ETYP
====

Equipment Type

## Format

Name | Type | Info
-----|------|-----
Equipment Type | int32 | Enum - see below for values.
 
### ETYP Enum Values

Value | Meaning
------|--------
-1 | None
0 | Big Guns
1 | Energy Weapons
2 | Small Guns
3 | Melee Weapons
4 | Unarmed Weapon
5 | Thrown Weapons
6 | Mine
7 | Body Wear
8 | Head Wear
9 | Hand Wear
10 | Chems
11 | Stimpack
12 | Food
13 | Alcohol
