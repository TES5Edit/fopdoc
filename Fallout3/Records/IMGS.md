The `rgb` data types above are apparently float32 triplets, with the first being for red, the second for green and the third for blue.
 
#### Flag Values

Value | Meaning
------|--------
0x01 | Saturation
0x02 | Contrast
0x04 | Tint
0x08 | Brightness
