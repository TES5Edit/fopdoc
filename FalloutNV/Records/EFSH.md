#### Flag Values

Value | Meaning
------|--------
0x01 | No Membrane Shader
0x02 | ??
0x04 | ??
0x08 | No Particle Shader
0x10 | Edge Effect - Inverse
0x20 | Membrane Shader - Affect Skin Only

Value | Meaning

7 | Destination Alpha
8 | Destination Inverted Alpha

10 | Destimation Inverse Color
